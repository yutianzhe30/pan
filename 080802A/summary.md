Summary of
Interview 080802A
with Demberel
Demberel was born in Tsagaannuur sum of Selenge Aimag in 1928 and graduated the sum primary school and secondary school Number 
One in Ulaanbaatar and studied at mathematics faculty of the Mongolian National University.
He entered in school in his teens and it was mandatory for school children to stay in dormitories. At the dormitory children 
had a hot bath and their clothes were cleaned of lice by putting them outside.
During the war, the windows of the households were covered by black cloth in order to hide the light in the evenings.
At that time, children were interested in school and everything was provided by the school. Only one sheep was given to school 
for school payment.  The sum submitted the names of all children to the school and only those who wanted to go went to school.
